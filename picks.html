A note from Ariel

Hey There! These are my personal picks for the June 2nd primary. I've done a lot of research on each of these races and these are the candidates I feel best represent our community and our values. I encourage you all to check out the full guide and make your own decisions, but if you trust my judgment and just want a quick answer, here you go!

For the races where I'm flagging a "tough choice," I've added a little note explaining my thinking. Those are the ones where I genuinely went back and forth, so take those with a grain of salt and make up your own mind!

Reminder: The top 2 vote-getters in each race advance to November regardless of party. Your primary vote matters!
